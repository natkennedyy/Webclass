All About Machu Picchu, Peru!

The name “Machu Picchu” comes from the Quechua Indian language, meaning “old peak” or “old mountain.” This name is fitting for this land because of its abandoned features. Machu Picchu is the most visited tourist destination in Peru. There are many beautiful ruins to explore including temples and sanctuaries. The weather is mostly warm and gets cool at night. Machu Picchu is the picture perfect location for any tourist!

Things to do:

  • Hiking
  • Climbing
  • Take Pictures
  • Explore the ruins
